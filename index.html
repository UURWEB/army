National service formation

Valorian Division

The Valorian Division is the organized armed force of Valoria operating inside the larger U.U.R. military. It preserves Valorian military traditions, uniforms, language, and internal unit identity while serving under Union High Command during joint operations and national emergencies.

Valorian officers retain responsibility for the division's training and internal discipline, while strategic deployment is coordinated with the wider Armed Forces.

Military heritage

One legend remembered across the Armed Forces.

General Grizzly is honored as one of the defining names in U.U.R. military history and remains a symbol of discipline, endurance, and armored strength.

General of the Union Ground Forces

General Grizzly

The Iron Bear

General Grizzly became one of the most respected commanders in U.U.R. history through a reputation for unbreakable defensive lines, careful planning, and decisive armored counterattacks. Troops gave the general the name “Iron Bear” for standing firm in the Union’s hardest campaigns and refusing to surrender ground placed under their protection.

Military doctrine later credited Grizzly with helping transform separate regional forces into disciplined formations capable of operating as one Union force. Their legacy still influences how the U.U.R. trains, organizes, and deploys its ground units today.

“A line is strongest when every soldier knows the Union stands behind it.”
Strategic infrastructure

The military keeps the Union powered.

The U.U.R. Central Power Station is operated and protected by the Engineering & Power Corps. Its primary responsibility is to supply Central City, government districts, military facilities, and the wider Union transmission network.

Military oversight ensures that power generation, distribution, repairs, and emergency restoration continue even during war, natural disaster, or disruption to civilian systems. The station is treated as both a public utility and a national defense installation.
